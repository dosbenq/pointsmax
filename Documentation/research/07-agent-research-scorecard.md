## Scoring Guidance
- `Source Quality`: Are sources primary, current, and cited?
- `Depth`: Does analysis go beyond summaries to real tradeoffs?
- `Actionability`: Can engineering/product act immediately?
- `Factual Accuracy`: Are claims grounded and consistent?
- `Rework Needed`: How much PM cleanup was required?

## Routing Rule
- Prefer agents with high source quality and low rework for strategic research tasks.
