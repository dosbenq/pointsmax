# Research Charter

## Snapshot
- Date started: `2026-02-27`
- Owner: `PM + research agents`
- Product: `PointsMax`
- Goal: `Identify highest-impact features and GTM strategy based on evidence`

## Strategic Questions
1. Which user pain points are most urgent and underserved in points/travel tools?
2. Which feature bets can create the strongest differentiation for PointsMax?
3. Which bets are monetizable in the next 90 days?
4. How should we build an "expert context" system (YouTube + web) safely and effectively?

## Target Segments (Draft)
- Mainstream points holders with redemption anxiety.
- Enthusiast optimizers seeking speed and depth.
- India market users with limited local tooling.

## Non-Goals (Current Cycle)
- Broad B2B platform strategy.
- Long-horizon white-label productization.
- Generic blog-content expansion without measurable conversion intent.

## Decision Criteria
- User impact (activation, retention, trust).
- Revenue impact (affiliate + subscription).
- Build feasibility (time-to-value, complexity).
- Defensibility (hard to copy quickly).

## Success Metrics
- Improved first-session activation rate.
- Increase in successful "next action" clicks (book/share/alert/apply).
- Growth in conversion-qualified traffic and affiliate CTR.
- Reduced time-to-confidence for redemption decisions.
